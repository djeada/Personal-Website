üí° How to Use
  • Pheromones: Green trails mark paths to food sources, blue trails lead back home
  • Ant Behavior: Ants use three sensors (left, center, right) to detect and follow pheromone gradients
  • Colony Size: Adjust the number of ants foraging (1-200)
  • Pheromone Strength: Control how much pheromone ants deposit on each step
  • Evaporation Rate: Higher values make trails persist longer (0.90-1.00)
  • Obstacles: Click and drag on the canvas to create barriers that ants must navigate around
  • Sensors: Toggle sensor visualization to see how ants detect pheromones
