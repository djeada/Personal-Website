Ant Colony Simulation

Watch ants forage for food using pheromone trails to communicate with their colony. This simulation demonstrates how simple rules and chemical signals lead to emergent intelligent behavior in ant colonies.

  • Ants leave pheromone trails - green for food, blue for home
  • Pheromones evaporate over time, creating efficient paths
  • Click and drag on the canvas to draw obstacles
  • Adjust colony size, pheromone strength, and evaporation rate with the controls below
