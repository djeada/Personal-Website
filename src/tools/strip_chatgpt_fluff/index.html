Need Help?
  • Paste or type your ChatGPT-generated text into the editor.
  • Drag and drop text files (.txt, .md) to load them directly.
  • Click "Process Text" or press Ctrl+Enter to clean the text.
  • Use the sidebar options to customize which transformations are applied.
  • Click preset buttons (Aggressive/Moderate/Conservative) for quick configurations.
  • Use the Search & Replace option to find and replace specific text patterns.
  • Track character, word, and line counts in the stats bar.
  • Use Ctrl+Z to undo and Ctrl+Y to redo changes.
  • Click "Copy" to copy the result or "Download" to save as a text file.
  • View your editing history in the collapsible History panel.
