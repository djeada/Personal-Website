Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is a popular arcade-style game where the goal is to fly your bird through obstacles without hitting them. Tap or click to make the bird fly higher.

  • To fly the bird, press the space bar or click.
  • Avoid hitting the pipes or the ground.
  • Press P to pause/resume; Esc to restart.
  • Press M to mute/unmute. On mobile, double-tap to pause.
