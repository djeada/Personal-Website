Snake

Snake is a classic game that was developed in the 1970's. The goal of the game is to collect as many apples as possible without hitting the walls or your own body.

  • To move the snake, use the arrow keys.
  • To pause the game, press the space bar.
  • To restart the game, press the escape key.
