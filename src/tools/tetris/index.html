Tetris

In Tetris, strategically rotate and arrange falling tetrominoes to create complete horizontal lines without gaps. As you clear lines, the game increases in speed, testing your reflexes and strategy. Enjoy endless hours of engaging gameplay and see how high you can score in this addictive and timeless puzzle game!

  • To move the block use the left and right arrow keys.
  • To rotate the block use the up arrow key.
  • To drop the block to the bottom of the grid use the space bar.
  • To restart the game use the escape key.
