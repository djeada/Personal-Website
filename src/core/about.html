About me

As a software developer, I have gained extensive experience and knowledge over the past decade through exploring various technologies. While there is an abundance of materials available, it can be difficult to find high-quality resources, especially on more advanced topics. In an effort to give back to the community and help others avoid the same struggles, I try to share my own insights and learning experiences as much as possible.

Throughout my professional career, I have not only written software but also prepared a large amount of technical documentation and tutorials for other developers. This has been an essential part of my personal and professional growth, as the feedback I have received from my colleagues has helped me to improve and expand my skills. This website is a compilation of my non-confidential work and learning experiences, and serves as a summary of everything I have done and learned thus far.
