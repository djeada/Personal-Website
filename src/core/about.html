About me

I'm a software developer living in Germany. In the last ten years, I've gone through various technologies, explored extensively, and learnt a great deal. The amount of materials we currently have at our fingertips is enormous, but the quality is not always there. It's especially difficult to find quality content when looking for information on more advanced topics. This is where I come in. I try to give back to the community as much as I can so that others do not have to go through the same struggles I did.

Throughout my professional career, I not only wrote software but also prepared a large amount of technical documentation and tutorials for other developers. I was able to grow much faster than I could have otherwise due to the feedback I received from my collegues. That website is essentially a summary of everything I've done and learned that isn't confidential.
